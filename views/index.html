How to Play

1. Create an account or login using your existing account details.

2. Enter your predictions for the different aspects of the six games (e.g. Score Result, Match Winner First player to score each week, etc).

3. Sit back, relax and keep an eye out for the results at the end of the week! Correct predictions will earn you points and help you rise up the leaderboards.
